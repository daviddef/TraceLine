# Privacy Policy — TraceLine

**Last updated: 16 July 2026**

## The short version

TraceLine does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There is no
account, no analytics, no advertising, and no server to send anything to. The game works
entirely offline.

## What the app stores

TraceLine saves your progress — level stars, high scores, and your chosen theme — using
`UserDefaults`, Apple's standard local storage. This data:

- never leaves your device,
- is not readable by the developer or anyone else,
- is deleted when you delete the app.

## Game Center

If you are signed in to Game Center, TraceLine submits your score to the leaderboard and
reports achievements. That data is handled by Apple under
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/), not by this app, and it is
tied to your Game Center identity rather than anything TraceLine knows about you.

Game Center is entirely optional. Declining to sign in has no effect on gameplay.

## What the app does not do

- No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
- No advertising or ad identifiers
- No tracking across apps or websites
- No in-app purchases
- No collection of contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers
- No data sold or shared with third parties

## Children

TraceLine is suitable for all ages and collects nothing from anyone, children included.

## Changes

Any change to this policy will be published on this page with an updated date.
